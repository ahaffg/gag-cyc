Croeso | Welcome

We're so glad you're here! This is your online hub to find out about voluntary opportunities in Ceredigion. Wether you want to make new friends, give back to the community or build relevant experience for your CV or field of study, chances are we can match you with an organisation or charity that would love to hear from you! So take a look around and begin your volunteering journey today!

Environment

Do you care deeply about the environment? Would you like to keep the local area safe and accesable for the comminuity and wildlife? If so, we've teamed up with local organisations like Tir Coed, Surfers Against Sewage and Eco Soc and have many rewarding opportunities available for all ages and levels of ability, click below to see out latest opportunities.

People
