Our Volunteers

We've been so lucky to work with some amazing people who havegiven their time to worthwhile causes in the area. Come and meet some of them and get inspired to start your own volunteering journey.

Mair and The Clean Crew

I found myself at a loss after retirement. Knitting and reading Mills and Boon just was not for me. Having been active all my life (I played netball for the county as a youngster and ran several clubs throughout the years) I did not want to spend the rest of my days sitting in an armchair! The clean crew have given me a new drive and I have enjoyed meeting people of all ages and from all walks of life. I have made friends and I am helping to keep our wonderful town beautiful, seems like a great deal to me!

Nico and Joanna

We are both second year Computer Science students at Aberystwyth University. We love the area and wanted to get to know the community beyond the campus. We saw the advert posted by Hafan Y Waun that was looking for people to provide simple tech lessons for the elderly. We've loved our time here! One of our favourite residents is Joe who we've taught basic iPad use so he can enjoy his favourite books and read to his grandchildren via skype. We thoroughly recommend undertaking a volunteering opportunity, especially as cups of tea are provided!

Sara

I am about to finish my art degree this year and I will begin a PGCE in September. I thought it would be a good idea to get some experience working with young children, so I've been holding art classes at Mudiad Meithrin for the past month. Its been very rewarding and its really helped me as I now have expreience and references I can add to my CV.
