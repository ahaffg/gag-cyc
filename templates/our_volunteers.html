Nico and Joanna

We are both second year Computer Science students at Aberystwyth University. We love the area, but wanted to get to know the compunity beyond the campus. We saw the advert posted by Hafan Y Waun that was looking for people to provide simple tech lessons for the elderly. We've loved our time here! One of our favourite residents is Joe who we've taught basic iPad use so he can emjoy his favourite books and read to his grandchildren via skype. We thoroughly recommend undertaking a volunteering opportunity, especially as cups of tea are provided!
